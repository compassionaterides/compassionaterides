What does Compassionate Rides represent?

Compassionate Rides represents quite a lot. For starters, Compassionate Rides represents a group of people who are willing to help their community, a group of people who enjoy being the reason someone is smiling. But, Compassionate Rides doesn't just represent a group of active members of society, Compassionate Rides goes way deeper than just one group of people. Compassionate Rides represents how no matter how unfortunate, different, or even how old you are, you will always have people whom are willing to help you with all your transportation needs.

What age do we hire?

We begin to hire at the age of 16 but only for summer internships. But a full-time or part-time job we hire at 18.
